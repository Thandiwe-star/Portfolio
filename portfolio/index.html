My Background and Goals

My journey

My journey in IT started when I became amazed by how simple code could turn into something powerful. I still remember back in 2024, when I was doing my Higher Certificate in ICT , the first time I wrote basic HTML and saw my own webpage appear on the screen. It honestly felt like magic!

That moment made me fall in love with technology and inspired me to learn more about web design, which is why I decided to continue with a Diploma in ICT.

As I learned more, I got interested in how all the parts of a website work together  the layout, design, and how people interact with it.

I also learned about making websites accessible for everyone, including people with disabilities. I believe a good design isn’t just about how it looks, but also how easy it is to use. So whenever I work on a site, I always aim to make it both beautiful and simple to navigate.  

Right now, I’m focused on improving my skills in front-end frameworks like React. My long-term dream is to become a certified UX designer who uses research and creativity to build digital products that truly make people’s lives easier.

My Goals

Short-Term Goal:

Right now, my main goal is to learn more about front-end libraries like React. I want to understand how to create websites that are interactive and respond to users in real time. Learning these tools will help me build websites that are not only  functional but also enjoyable for people to use.

Mid-Term Goal:

 In the next few years, I want to take on more challenging projects that push me to grow my skills. I also hope to work with other designers and developers to bring creative ideas to life. Another goal of mine is to gain experience in user testing, so I can see how real people use my designs and improve them based on their feedback.

Long-Term Goal:

 My biggest dream is to become a certified UX designer. I want to understand how people use digital products and use that knowledge to design solutions that truly make a difference. Whether it’s improving accessibility, making things easier to use,  or helping people in their everyday lives, I want my work to have a positive impact. I’m very excited about my future in the IT field and eager to keep learning and growing in this fast-changing industry. I know there’s still a lot to discover, but I’m ready to face new challenges and take every opportunity that comes my way.
